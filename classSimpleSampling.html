Detailed Description

Naive Simple Sampling.

This generates each sample fro scratch and performs "normal" simple sampling. This can be faster for easy to generate realization as normal random walks, but may be slower than Markov Chain Monte Carlo (eg. Metropolis) for, eg., SAW.
